ดนตรีไทย

Immerse yourself in Thailand's rich musical heritage with our authentic traditional programs. Learn from master musicians who are dedicated to preserving and teaching Thailand's musical traditions.

ดนตรีตะวันตก

Study classical composition and performance under internationally acclaimed instructors. Our curriculum covers theory, history, and performance techniques from the Baroque to Contemporary periods.

รายละเอียด

ดนตรีพื้นบ้าน

Master modern genres and techniques with our industry-experienced faculty. Explore jazz, rock, pop, and electronic music production while building practical skills for today's music industry.

เกี่ยวกับหลักสูตร

Founded in 1995, our institution has been at the forefront of music education in Thailand for over three decades. We combine traditional teaching methods with innovative approaches to nurture well-rounded musicians.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include professional recording studios, practice rooms equipped with premium instruments, and performance venues designed with optimal acoustics.
